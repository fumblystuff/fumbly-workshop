Welcome to the Fumbly Workshop

Fumbly Workshop is the woodworking blog of John M. Wargo. For the last 25 years, I built my woodworking skills and built out my workshop; I created this site to allow me to share what I've learned.

Learnings, insights, rants, & more.

Articles

This is the blog portion of the site; here I share something I learned like when I create an interesting jig, learn a new skill, or make something cool that I think may interest readers. My primary goal is to share tips and tricks I've learned along the way to, hopefully, accelerate your growth as a woodworker or at least help you get past a particular problem.
